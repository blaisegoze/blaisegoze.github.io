Build-A-Pet

In a dismal future, Build-A-Bear has evolved. Children no longer desire a fluffy bear for comfort and security, instead they seek something real, composed of a conglomeration of actual and imaginary creatures.

The work was created in Photoshop and the breakdown was made in After Effects.
