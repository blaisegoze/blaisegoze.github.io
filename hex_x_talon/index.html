Hex x Talon

Hex x Talon is a product video collaboration between Crazy Creek and Osprey with the Hex 2.0™ chair and the Talon™ 33 backpack.

Filmed on an Sony a7S III, edited in Premiere, and the graphics were animated in After Effects.
